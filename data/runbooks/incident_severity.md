# Incident Severity Guide

Synthetic sample runbook for portfolio/demo purposes.

SEV-1: broad customer-facing outage, data loss risk, or security emergency.

SEV-2: material production impact for a critical service, elevated 5xx errors, severe latency, or significant authentication/checkout degradation.

SEV-3: degraded background processing, queue backlog, partial feature degradation, or risk of customer impact without confirmed outage.

SEV-4: low impact, informational, or non-urgent investigation.
